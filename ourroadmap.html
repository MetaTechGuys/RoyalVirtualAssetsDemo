At Roial Virtual Assets (RVA), we are driven by a clear and ambitious vision: to deliver a next-generation decentralised finance ecosystem that combines transparency, security, and efficiency. Our roadmap reflects a carefully planned, phased approach designed to ensure sustainable growth, technical excellence, and regulatory readiness. Below is a summary of our key milestones, achievements, and upcoming goals.

Phase 1 — Foundation and Development (Completed)
Company incorporation and legal structuring
Core team assembly — blockchain developers, finance professionals, legal and compliance advisors
Design and development of ICO/IDO platform
Development of multi-asset RVA Wallet
Completion of RVA Smart Chain architecture
Internal audits and security testing of completed products
Launch of official RVA website and communication channels

Phase 2 — Technical Integration and Ecosystem Expansion (Ongoing)
Expansion of developer tools and SDKs for RVA Smart Chain
Preparation of Smart Exchange architecture and liquidity framework
Integration of API and external liquidity providers
Partnerships with compliance and KYC/AML service providers
Beta testing of Smart Exchange with invited users and partners
Preparation of documentation for institutional and broker integrations

Phase 3 — Public Launch and User Acquisition (Upcoming)
Public launch of RVA Smart Exchange
Deployment of KYC/AML infrastructure for regulatory compliance
Onboarding of early adopters, partners, and institutional clients
Launch of global marketing and community outreach campaigns
Establishment of governance frameworks for community input and decentralised decision-making
Expansion of supported assets, trading pairs, and DeFi functionalities

Phase 4 — Growth, Innovation, and Sustainability (Future Plans)
Launch of staking, liquidity pools, and yield farming on the RVA Smart Chain
Continuous innovation in decentralised financial products
Expansion into new markets and jurisdictions
Integration with additional blockchains and cross-chain bridges
Regular security audits, compliance reviews, and platform optimisations
Establishment of RVA Foundation to support open-source development and ecosystem grants
